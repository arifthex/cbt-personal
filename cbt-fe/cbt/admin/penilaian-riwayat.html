Penilaian Essay – Rahmanda Safitri

Berikan skor pada setiap jawaban essay siswa. Skor per soal adalah 0-20.

📘 Soal Essay #1

Pertanyaan:

What is the main difference between "present perfect" and "past simple" tense? Provide one example for each.

Jawaban Siswa:

Past simple is for action finished in the past. Present perfect is for action in the past but has connection to the present. Example past simple: I went to Bali last year. Example present perfect: I have visited Bali.

📘 Soal Essay #2

Describe your favorite holiday in at least 50 words.

My favorite holiday is Eid al-Fitr. I can meet my big family from my father and mother side. We go to grandfather house together. My mother cooks very delicious food like opor ayam and ketupat. We eat together and talk a lot. I get 'angpao' from my uncle and aunt. I am very happy.
